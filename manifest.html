Declaration of Nutopia

Nutopia has no land, no boundaries, no passports — only people.

This page is a respectful paraphrase of the 1973 declaration and its spirit: a conceptual nation open to everyone, founded on love and peace.

Principles

  • No borders · No ownership · No hierarchy
  • Citizens are ambassadors
  • Consciousness is the only visa
  • Flag of white surrender to peace
